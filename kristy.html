Spicy tofu stew

Recipe

Serves 1 or 2

  • 8 large dried anchovies
  • 5 oz. of radish
  • Dried kelp
  • 2 tbsp. hot pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp. sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup meat of choice
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup well-fermented kimchi
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 1 tube of soft tofu
  • 1 egg

    • Directions

    Special thanks to Maangchi

    1. Put dried anchovies, radish, dried kelp, and 4 cups of water in a pot. Cover and boil over medium high heat for 10 minutes until it starts boiling.
    2. Reduce the heat to low and boil for another 20 minutes.
    3. Remove from the heat and strain. It will make about 2 cups of stock.
    4. Combine the hot pepper flakes and the sesame oil in a small bowl and mix well.
    5. If available, heat up a earthenware pot (or regular pot) on the stove for 3-4 minutes.
    6. Add vegetable oil, onion, and garlic. Stir for 1 minute.
    7. Add meat and stir fry until no longer pink.
    8. Add kimchi and keep stirring for a minute. Add anchovy stock. Cover and cook for 7 minutes over medium heat.
    9. Add salt and sugar and mix.
    10. Cut the tofu and place it into the pot. Break up the tofu in the pot.
    11. Put the hot pepper mixture on top and spread it.
    12. Crack the egg. Let it bubble and sizzle for 1 minute.
    13. Garnish with green onion and feel free to have rice on the side.
    14. Serve!
